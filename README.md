## Research Project: AI-Powered SEO Content Production

### What I Collected
For this research project, I identified 10 leading experts in 
AI-powered SEO content production. For each expert I collected:
- Their most recent and relevant LinkedIn posts
- YouTube video transcripts with key insights extracted

For YouTube transcripts, I used youtubetranscript.com as a free 
transcript extraction method — a tool that accesses YouTube's 
transcript API to retrieve full video transcripts quickly and 
at scale across all 10 experts.

All material is organized in the /research/ folder.

### Why I Chose These Experts
I specifically looked for practitioners — people who actually 
implement AI SEO strategies, not just write about them. I prioritized 
experts who are active on LinkedIn and YouTube, have real client 
experience, and are sharing data-backed insights rather than just 
opinions.

### Experts and Why I Chose Them

1. **Aleyda Solís** — International SEO consultant running Orainti. 
   One of the most cited voices on AI search and GEO. Hosts Crawling 
   Mondays on YouTube and writes the SEOFOMO newsletter.

2. **Neil Patel** — Co-founder of NP Digital. 1.5M YouTube subscribers. 
   Publishes data-driven research on AI content strategy and citation 
   optimization.

3. **Garrett Sussman** — Director of Marketing at iPullRank. Architect 
   of SEO Week conference. Combines psychology and AI search strategy 
   in a way few others do.

4. **Michael King** — Founder of iPullRank, 2X Search Marketer of the 
   Year. Builds open-source SEO tools and pushes the industry forward 
   with technical AI search research.

5. **Edward Sturm** — SEO practitioner and podcaster with 1,035+ daily 
   episodes. Known for practical, no-fluff takes on AI content abuse 
   and brand building.

6. **Amanda Natividad** — Chief Evangelist at SparkToro and co-author 
   of Zero Click Marketing. Leading voice on zero-click search and 
   what AI means for content strategy.

7. **Lily Ray** — VP of SEO & AI Search at Amsive. Debunks GEO myths 
   with data. One of the clearest thinkers on what has actually changed 
   vs what is just hype in AI search.

8. **Kevin Indig** — Growth Advisor to Airbnb, Meta, Shopify. Publishes 
   The Growth Memo newsletter. Conducted the first usability study of 
   AI Overviews.

9. **Milosz Krasinski** — Managing Director at Chilli Fruit Web 
   Consulting. Specializes in AI SEO and LLM optimization for SaaS 
   and B2B brands.

10. **Jason M. Lemkin** — Founder of SaaStr. 308K LinkedIn followers. 
    Sharing real data on how AI agents are replacing GTM teams and 
    what that means for content and marketing.
