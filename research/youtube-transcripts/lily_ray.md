## Key Insights

**On GEO vs SEO:**
Good SEO is really the same thing as good GEO. Google confirmed this. 
Many supposedly new GEO tactics are just traditional SEO best practices 
with new names. Chunking is just good formatting. FAQs and structured 
data we've been doing since 2011.

**On AI Traffic Reality:**
LLMs drive about 1-2% of traffic compared to organic search driving 
30-50%. Most ChatGPT users still use Google heavily. After using ChatGPT, 
average Google sessions per week actually increased from 10.5 to 12.6.

**On RAG and SEO:**
LLMs use Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) — they retrieve info from 
search engines before generating responses. Every URL in an LLM output 
comes from Google or Bing. SEO has never been more important.

**On New AI Search KPIs:**
Stop just tracking clicks and rankings. New metrics include: branded 
impressions, share of voice in AI search, direct/organic clicks to 
homepage, AI search visibility by topic, and traffic from LLM referrers.

**On What Actually Works:**
- Answer all branded questions clearly on your website
- Earn brand mentions through digital PR
- Be present on Reddit and YouTube authentically
- Keep content updated with genuinely new information
- Think about personalization and regional content
