# Aleyda Solís — YouTube Transcript

## Video: AI Search, GEO & SEO Strategy
Source: https://youtu.be/pqrwpXpMM6s?si=pAXY3YeE7as-tqiO

## Key Insights

**On AI crawlers:**
AI crawlers don't render JavaScript at all. If you rely on client-side 
JavaScript for critical content, AI crawlers won't see it. You also need 
to make sure your hosting isn't blocking AI bots.

**On SEO vs GEO:**
The principles of SEO remain valid in AI search. LLMs are just a new 
search channel — not a completely new discipline. Leverage your existing 
SEO team rather than creating a separate GEO role.

**On content strategy:**
Stop creating fluffy, generic top-of-funnel content. Instead focus on:
- Industry insights, research, stats, trends
- Case studies and thought leadership
- Competitor comparisons and FAQs
- On-brand content that highlights your unique selling propositions

**On brand mentions:**
Mentions now matter as much as backlinks. AI search rewards brands with 
real community signals. Monitor your share of voice and sentiment versus 
competitors across AI platforms.

**On AI search opportunity:**
Startups with strong community presence are already getting cited in 
ChatGPT and Perplexity even without strong backlinks — because they 
focused on genuine product marketing.
