# Garrett Sussman — YouTube Transcript

## Video: Psychology, AI Search & Relevance Engineering
Source: https://youtu.be/Dh1i_t1kchs?si=YAF_5eNWHYJO7bNa

## Key Insights

**On AI Search Terminology:**
The debate between SEO, GEO, AEO, and AI search isn't fully resolved. 
iPullRank uses "relevance engineering" as an umbrella term. The real 
issue is clients don't know what to ask for — agreeing on terminology 
helps drive business forward.

**On Query Fan Out:**
When someone searches, AI generates 20-30 synthetic queries behind the 
scenes. Each one is an opportunity to have your content cited. You need 
to create more granular content than ever before to give yourself as many 
chances as possible to be pulled into AI answers.

**On Confirmation Bias in Search:**
How you phrase a search influences the outcome. AI search makes this 
worse through hyper-personalization. Marketers need to be aware of how 
their own biases — and their audience's — shape search behavior.

**On Content Strategy:**
Think omni-channel — not just your website. Are you showing up in 
Reddit, YouTube, LinkedIn? Every platform is a "raffle ticket" to be 
pulled into an AI answer. Diversify your presence across platforms.

**On Replacing Marketers:**
AI will eventually replace a lot of marketing work, but right now smart 
marketers who can connect dots, ask the right questions, and build 
personalized strategies are still irreplaceable.
