# Amanda Natividad — YouTube Transcript

## Video: Zero-Click Web, AI Search & What Actually Works Now
Source: https://youtu.be/yepb95llLro?si=CDFIPNQNSO2otL_i

## Key Insights

**On Zero-Click Searches:**
In the first 4 months of 2026, 68% of Google searches ended without a 
click. In 2019 it was 49%, in 2024 it was 60%. AI Overviews now appear 
on 20%+ of searches and cut click-through rates by almost 60%.

**On Who Is Still Winning:**
Sites that offer a product/service and let users complete a task win. 
84% of winning sites let you actually do something. Pure information 
sites and affiliate sites are losing. Build a destination, not a doorway.

**On Content Strategy:**
Stop writing to be clicked. Write to be cited. Your content's new job 
is to be the source the machine trusts. Publish facts only you can 
publish clearly so AI cites you correctly.

**On Traffic as a Metric:**
Stop using traffic as your north star. Companies are seeing traffic 
down and revenue up. Replace traffic with a correlation dashboard — 
track branded search, direct visits, and revenue instead.

**On AI Commerce:**
Adobe found traffic from AI to retailers grew almost 400% in a year and 
converts 42% better than any other traffic source. The human a machine 
hands you is your best visitor.

**5 Action Steps:**
1. Fire traffic as your north star metric
2. Do audience research — find where your people actually pay attention
3. Stop shipping commodity content — build a destination with tasks
4. Publish facts only you can publish so machines cite you correctly
5. Keep doing SEO for branded terms, local intent, and high-intent queries
