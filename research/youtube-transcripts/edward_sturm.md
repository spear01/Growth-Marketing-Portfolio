# Edward Sturm — YouTube Transcript

## Video: AI SEO, Scaled Content Abuse & Brand Building
Source: https://youtu.be/1CVkuVqMZBQ?si=Z0372-8UBOPIn_fN

## Key Insights

**On Scaled Content Abuse:**
Google introduced a Scaled Content Abuse policy in March 2024. Sites that 
use AI to mass-produce pages see traffic spike for 4-6 months then drop 
80-90%. Even Shopify got hit because it stretched its authority too far.

**On AI + Human Content Mix:**
Don't fire your content team. Use AI to get the first draft in 5-10 
minutes, then have humans edit, verify facts, and add brand voice. Aim 
for 80% human, 20% AI — not the other way around.

**On Programmatic SEO vs Scaled Abuse:**
Good programmatic SEO scales something unique that doesn't exist on the 
web yet — your proprietary data, opinions, research. Bad programmatic 
SEO just scales generic AI-generated content at speed.

**On Brand Building as the Real SEO:**
Companies that will win SEO in 3 years are focused on brand building 
over SEO tactics. Omni-channel presence, branded traffic, and a 
structured funnel (top, middle, bottom) are what actually matters 
long-term.

**On Social Profiles:**
Social profiles are becoming more important as trust signals. Anyone 
can buy a domain, but social profiles require minimal identity 
verification — making them stronger signals of legitimacy to Google.
