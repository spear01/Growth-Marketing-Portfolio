# Kevin Indig — YouTube Transcript

## Video: AI Overviews, Search KPIs & The Future of Organic Growth
Source: https://youtu.be/qujABKOAThA?si=EA0rkUSfckXwi7JO

## Key Insights

**On AI Overviews Replacing Product Grids:**
AI overviews are starting to replace product grids in Google search 
results. Image carousels are growing. Reddit discussions and forums 
feature is actually decreasing in e-commerce search results.

**On Clicks vs Influence:**
Only 1% of users click on citations in AI overviews. But 80% of 
final clicks still go to organic results. Search is no longer about 
clicks — it's about answers and influence.

**On Trust as the New Ranking Signal:**
Users now judge results by trust first, relevance second. They check 
the brand/domain before deciding if it answers their question. Being 
known before someone searches is becoming critical.

**On Different Chatbots Citing Different Sources:**
- ChatGPT relies heavily on Wikipedia and Reddit
- Perplexity relies heavily on Reddit and Google
- LinkedIn Newsletters and YouTube have outsized impact on ChatGPT

**On Content Structure:**
Use descriptive headings as semantic tools, not design tools. Add 
key takeaways, table of contents, and FAQ sections. Simple, clear 
content with active voice performs better with both users and AI.

**On New SEO KPIs:**
Move from clicks to leading indicators: rankings, share of voice, 
branded impressions, and brand recall. The old funnel of search → 
click → convert is broken. Accept the fractured funnel.
