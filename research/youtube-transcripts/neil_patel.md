# Neil Patel — YouTube Transcript

## Video: 5 Strategies to Rank in AI-Powered Search 2026
Source: https://youtu.be/tMBdA2gkXgk?si=wLrdboz45I9VV7qh

## Key Insights

**Strategy 1 — Topical Depth over Keywords:**
No consistent correlation between keyword density and ranking. Topical 
authority is now the strongest on-page ranking factor. Build pillar pages 
linked to subtopics and use semantic variety to show genuine expertise.

**Strategy 2 — Author and Brand Authority (E-E-A-T for AI):**
Average content from an authority outperforms great content from a nobody. 
AI asks not just "what does this page say?" but "who said it and can we 
trust it?" Get featured on podcasts, blogs, and news outlets. Even 
unlinked mentions strengthen your authority footprint.

**Strategy 3 — Optimize for AI Summaries:**
AI platforms drove 9.7% of B2B revenue and 11.4% of B2C revenue despite 
driving less than 1% of traffic — because conversion rates are very high. 
Write in direct Q&A format. Structure content with short paragraphs, 
bullet points, and subheadings.

**Strategy 4 — Structured Data and AI-Friendly Content:**
AI skims content like humans do. Use schema markup (FAQ, HowTo, Review). 
Add multiple formats — videos, visuals, charts. Use numbered lists, 
stats, and tables. AI platforms love blogs with images and data 
visualizations.

**Strategy 5 — Rethink SEO Workflow for Generative Search:**
Track citations, not just rankings. Test your content by pasting it into 
ChatGPT and asking it to summarize. Blog consistently — AI platforms cite 
blogs more than any other content type. Companies that kept blogging saw 
AI traffic and total sales go up even as traditional organic traffic dropped.
