# Jason M. Lemkin — YouTube Transcript

## Video: AI Agents Replacing Sales Teams & The Future of GTM
Source: https://youtu.be/I-R1bc1rlFs?si=gqxmcT5O9LR0MnyN

## Key Insights

**On Replacing Sales Teams with AI:**
SaaStr went from 10 GTM humans to 1.2 humans and 20 AI agents — doing 
the same revenue. AI agents work all night, weekends, and holidays. 
Email-based SDRs and inbound lead qualifiers will be mostly extinct 
within 12 months.

**On What AI Can and Can't Replace:**
AI replaces the midpack and mediocre. The best humans get superpowers 
from AI. Field sales, enterprise deals, and high-dollar negotiations 
still need humans. But if you can close on a text message, AI can 
close it too.

**On Training AI Agents:**
Agents don't work out of the box. Take your best salesperson's email 
copy and use it as a template. Train the agent every day for 30 days 
correcting mistakes. By day 30 it becomes a version of your best rep.

**On How to Stay Employed:**
Pick one agentic tool. Deploy it yourself. Train it, ingest data, 
iterate daily. The people who do this become hyper-employable. 
Companies need GTM engineers who've actually done this hands-on.

**On The Plays vs The Playbooks:**
All the plays still work — outbound, webinars, podcasts, events. 
But the playbooks are broken. Don't follow old GTM playbooks. 
Embrace the plays, adapt the execution with AI.
