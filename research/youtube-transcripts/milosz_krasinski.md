# Milosz Krasinski — YouTube Transcript

## Video: Link Building, AI Search & SEO Strategy
Source: https://youtu.be/fXqoPS1mk1g?si=ttvcagGmoBoFsxZ7

## Key Insights

**On Link Building Fundamentals:**
Guest posts, link insertions, and digital PR still work when done 
correctly. SEO is a long-term compound interest game — consistent 
monthly investment beats sporadic big bets. Every link you build is 
a digital asset that stays with you, unlike PPC spend.

**On ChatGPT and Content:**
ChatGPT uses Bing as its data source (Microsoft partnership). The 
more fresh content you publish — especially listing-style content 
where you position yourself as a top option — the more you influence 
AI probability-based recommendations.

**On DR80 Misconception:**
High DR links look impressive but often don't pass real SEO value 
because they're deep in page hierarchy and not well internally linked. 
Focus on relevant, well-indexed links that point to converting pages — 
not just homepage ego boosts from digital PR.

**On PPC + SEO Together:**
PPC tells you which keywords actually convert — use that data to 
inform your SEO strategy. Having both SEO and paid rankings 
simultaneously exponentially increases click-through rates.

**On Brand Touch Points:**
SEO, link building, PPC, email, and social media are all touch points. 
The more touch points you create around your brand, the more likely 
customers will remember and trust you when they're ready to buy.
