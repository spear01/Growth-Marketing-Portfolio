# Edward Sturm — LinkedIn Posts

## Post 1
If you want to learn how to absolutely tank the traffic for your blog, 
pay attention to what this company did.

They put up 2,815 templated posts in 16 months. Google has an entire 
Scaled Content Abuse policy calling out the use of generative AI tools, 
which this company was using.

Problems with their approach:
- Self-promotional listicles always placed themselves at number one
- Top section about themselves was 4-7x longer than competitors
- Ton of CTAs promoting themselves on each page
- Same template repeated for everything
- Optimized for conversion funnel, not for the searcher's questions

The result: Their blog traffic fell off a cliff (1.19M to 28K visitors).

Key lesson: AI content at scale without genuine value destroys SEO. 
Content must answer the searcher's actual questions, not your funnel.
