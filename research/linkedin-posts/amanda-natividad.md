## Post 1
"What's our AI visibility like?" If my CEO or CMO asked me that, 
here's what I'd do.

First, run your brand through HubSpot's AEO Grader. Then summarize 
what stands out.

Key findings from running SparkToro through it:
- AI correctly identified SparkToro as a niche audience research tool
- Picked up on their biggest advantage: credibility with marketing practitioners
- Competitive set was messy — SparkToro compared to Semrush, Ahrefs, 
  Similarweb, Brandwatch (not all direct competitors)
- SparkToro shows up 24% in ChatGPT and Perplexity, but just 12.5% in Gemini

What to do with this info:
1. Find what AI is already getting right about your brand
2. Find the misconceptions — wrong category, wrong comparisons
3. Turn the gaps into priorities for website copy, content, PR, and 
   sales enablement
4. Run the analysis again in 1-2 months to see what moved

Key insight: If AI believes something off about you, odds are part of 
your audience does too.
