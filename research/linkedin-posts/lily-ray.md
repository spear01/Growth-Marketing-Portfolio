## Post 1
Here's a little hack to quickly convert SEO keywords into relevant 
AI prompts at scale directly in Google Sheets.

Use the =AI or =Gemini function to convert SEO keywords into associated 
ways that users might type (or ask out loud) that same question into 
an AI assistant.

It allows you to take keywords with real search volume (from Ahrefs, 
Semrush, Similarweb, etc.) and quickly convert them into AI prompts 
at scale.

Google Sheets Prompt:
=AI("You are converting a terse SEO search keyword into the natural-language 
query a real person would actually type or speak to an AI assistant like 
ChatGPT, Gemini, or a voice assistant. Rewrite the keyword as a single 
conversational question or request that preserves the exact search intent 
and intent type. Phrase it the way someone would genuinely ask out loud 
in a full sentence — not in keyword shorthand.", A2)
