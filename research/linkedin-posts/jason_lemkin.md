## Post 1
The contact-us form is the most expensive lazy decision in B2B.

Someone lands on your site ready to talk. They hit a form. A human 
round-robins it. A rep follows up on a delay. Two or three days later, 
if the lead is still warm, a conversation happens. Usually it isn't 
still warm anymore.

SaaStr replaced theirs with one inbound AI agent. For a single event 
it booked 614 meetings at roughly $85K average ticket, across ~2.25M 
sessions and ~402K interactions. With just 3 people on the team.

What the agent does:
- Routes every lead to the right rep based on actual close data
- Runs re-engagement campaigns for people who don't finish
- Recovers tickets and sponsors a human would never circle back to
- Handles discounting inside guardrails

ICONIQ's 2026 data shows conversion down 5-10 points across the funnel 
and sales cycles 3-4 weeks longer. A slow form isn't a minor 
inefficiency anymore. It's where deals die.
